About Us

Our Mission

We hold snails deep in our hearts and we are passionate about helping them find love. We think snails are amazing creatures and they deserve to find love just like us. To us, snails are more than just bugs and we hope the rest of the world can see that. We had this idea to help snails find love while walking early in the morning and we saw a lonley snail scooting across the sidewalk. This sparked a fire in our hearts to help all the snails of the world not have to cross the sidewalks alone.

Our goal in making this website is to bring love to all the lonley snails of the world. We want the snails using our website to find love like us humans find love. When we came up with the idea to create ShellMatch we emphasized the snails ability to find love. Our goal is to allow all snails of all kinds to find love and be able to share a life full of love with thier ShellMate. We have made it our mission to make it possible for all the lonely snails in the world to find the love they have been longing for their whole life.
